Peanut Noodles

Description

Noodles dressed with a thick sauce that would be good on lots of things.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti, 1 serving
  • Sesame oil, 1 tbsp
  • Scallions, 1-2 stalks, chopped
  • Garlic, 1 clove, sliced
  • Ginger, 1/2 tsp, minced
  • Peanut butter, 2 tbsp
  • Soy sauce, 1/8 cup
  • Cider vinegar, 1/2 tbsp
  • Sugar, 1/2 tbsp
  • Red pepper flakes, 1/8 tsp or to taste
  • Hot water, 1/4 cup or to desired thickness

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in water until nearly done.
  2. Meanwhile, in a small skillet, cook onions in oil over medium-low heat until tender.
  3. Add ginger and garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Mix in remaining ingredients and remove from heat.
  5. Add pasta water to desired thickness.
  6. Toss with noodles and serve.
